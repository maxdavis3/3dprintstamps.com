Custom Stamp Services

Logo & Brand Stamps

Precise photopolymer stamps of company logos, monograms, and brand marks. Self-inking, pre-inked, and open-mount options. Up to 100mm × 60mm impression area for business use.

Address & Text Stamps

Return address and business information stamps in any font. Multi-line layouts with precise character spacing. Standard ink colors: black, blue, red, green, and purple.

03

Wax Seal Dies & Embossing Tools

Custom wax seal dies in brass-filled resin and photopolymer for letter sealing and packaging. Embossing folder dies for card making and paper art. Any crest, monogram, or custom design.

STAMP MATERIALS

Stamp Manufacturing Materials

Stamp material governs ink transfer quality, impression clarity, and service life — we match the material to your application.

  • ● Photopolymer resin — fine text to 0.3mm height
  • ● Red rubber — traditional stamp, oil-based inks
  • ● Microcell foam — self-inking refillable pad stamps
  • ● Brass-filled resin — wax seal die durability
  • ● Photopolymer clear — precise logo registration
  • ● Custom ink colors and dye formulations available
